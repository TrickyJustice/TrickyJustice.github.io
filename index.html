ABOUT ME




Hi there! I am Achint Soni, a junior undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur majoring in Electrical Engineering. I am most passionate about giving back to the community, and my goal is to pursue this passion within the field of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

Research

My main research interests are in Data Analytics, Machine learning and Artificial Intelligence. I have worked with machine learning in a wide range of research fields: social media analytics, robotics, or even biology.
I have performed sentiment analysis on tweets from Twitter to extract the demand for a particular product. I have also worked on speech recognition that was used in unmanned vehicles for its navigation.
My current research work includes classifying motor imagery brain signals to detect the movement of arms and legs. This model will then be added to a robotic wheelchair specially designed for disabled patients to help them reach a specified destination by just thinking about moving their arms or legs.
