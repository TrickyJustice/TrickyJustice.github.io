    About Me

    Educational Background

    I am a Research Masters (Thesis) student in Computer Science at the Cheriton School of Computer Science, University of Waterloo, advised by Prof. Sirisha Rambhatla and Prof. Charles Clarke.
    Previously, I received my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, where I was advised by Prof. Faiz Hamid and Prof. Laxmidhar Behera.

    Research Interests

  • Foundation models (including generative world models)
  • Image and video generation/editing
  • Multimodal (vision/language) understanding
  • Disentangled representation learning
